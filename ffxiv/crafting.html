What I did! (And some of what I should've done, but didn't)

Important to know: This strategy is intended for people that are leveling everything quickly and don't want to stop to farm gemstones or buy gemstone materials from the marketboard as much. This is NOT for leveling a single crafter at a time and waiting for leve allowances to do more. Use the collectible options when you can to maintain your leve allowances for the harder crafters. Weaver is a bitch, but Leatherworker is worse.
If you don't mind farming gemstones or buying materials, use the highest EXP options. If you are waiting for leve allowances between each crafter, use strictly leves since collectibles are not as good for EXP.
