Basic Text Commands

<flag> - Links selected map location
<pos> - Links your current position
<t> - Links your current target
/fc - Sends message to FC chat
/p - Sends message to Party chat
/sh - Sends message to Shout chat
/l1 - Sends message to linkshell 1.  Change number for different linkshells.
/cwl1 - Sends message to crossworld linkshell 1.  Change number for different CW linkshells.

Other Useful Macros

Hunt Train Scout

Change "lX" to whichever linkshell needs to recieve your train scouting locations. Be sure to remove any lines you don't want seeing the links to avoid early relinks.

/e <t><pos>
/p <t><pos>
/lX <t><pos>
/cwlX <t><pos>

Echo Refresh

To refresh your links to avoid losing them from your chat log

/e <flag>
